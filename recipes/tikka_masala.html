Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce with tender pieces of chicken. It's a flavorful and comforting meal that's perfect for any occasion.

This recipe combines marinated and grilled chicken with a delectable tomato and cream sauce. The fragrant spices and herbs used in this dish create a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds delighted.

Chicken Tikka Masala is often served with steamed rice or naan bread. It's a dish that's loved by people around the world and can be customized to your desired level of spiciness.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh pieces
  • Yogurt and spices for marination
  • Tomatoes and cream for the sauce
  • Onions, garlic, and ginger
  • Various spices and herbs

Steps to make Chicken Tikka Masala

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken pieces with yogurt and spices. Let it marinate for at least an hour.
  2. Grill or Broil: Cook the marinated chicken until done, either by grilling or broiling.
  3. Create the Sauce: In a separate pan, prepare a luscious tomato-based sauce with onions, garlic, ginger, and spices.
  4. Blend Chicken and Sauce: Combine the grilled chicken with the sauce and simmer until well-mixed and the sauce thickens.
  5. Adjust Spices: Adjust the level of spiciness to your preference by adding more or less of the chosen spices.
  6. Garnish: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves for a burst of flavor.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice or naan bread for a complete meal.
  8. Enjoy: Relish the delicious flavors of your homemade Chicken Tikka Masala.
